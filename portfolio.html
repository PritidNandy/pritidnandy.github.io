Forecasting UK Whisky and Gin Exports Under Tariffs and FTAs

🎯 Executive Summary

An end-to-end forecasting framework for UK whisky and gin exports to the United States and India, linking monthly HMRC trade flows with GDP, CPIH spirits inflation, and GBP exchange rates.

💼 The Business Problem

  • UK distilleries make multi-year capacity decisions while exports respond month-by-month to tariffs and economic cycles
  • Without forward-looking forecasting, producers risk over-producing into depressed demand or under-serving key markets
  • Trade negotiators lack quantitative models to assess FTA impact

💡 Business Impact

  • Market Expansion: Distilleries can dynamically route inventory to markets with most favorable economic conditions
  • Risk Mitigation: Quantifies product resilience to global inflation
  • Policy Influence: Provides data-driven estimates for trade negotiations

🔧 Methodology

Stack: Python, Pandas, Statsmodels (SARIMAX), XGBoost, Plotly/Dash

Approach: Harmonized HMRC CN8 trade data with ONS CPIH, IMF GDP, and BoE FX rates. Compared SARIMAX vs. XGBoost on MAE/RMSE.

📌 Key Insights

  • Whisky exports showed 0.68 elasticity to partner-country GDP
  • Under no-tariff scenario, exports would have been 14-22% higher during 2019-2021
  • Front-loaded FTA tariff reductions projected 31% export growth over 5 years

Global CO₂ Emission Analysis & Prediction

🎯 Executive Summary

A comprehensive regression analysis of passenger car emissions across Nordic countries to identify predominant vehicle characteristics driving greenhouse gases.

💼 The Business Problem

  • Automakers struggle to bridge gap between lab-tested and real-world emissions
  • Without understanding which variables contribute most to emissions, policies remain inefficient
  • Manufacturing strategies lack data-driven prioritization

💡 Business Impact

  • For Policymakers: Enables data-driven tax incentives rather than blanket regulations
  • For Manufacturers: Highlights engineering bottlenecks for targeted R&D investment

🔧 Methodology

Stack: Python, Scikit-learn, Seaborn/Matplotlib

Approach: Cleaned EEA datasets, generated correlation matrices, implemented multiple linear regression.

📌 Key Insights

  • Engine power and weight showed strongest correlation with CO₂ (r > 0.78)
  • Hybrid/electric vehicles reduced emissions by 62% on average
  • Country-level policy differences explained 23% of residual variance
